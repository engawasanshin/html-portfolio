The Best Movies According To Shio

My top3 movies of all time

The Shawshank Redemption

I love the importance of maintaining hope, the depiction of the scenery, and the changes in the relationships between the prisoners.

The Martian

I love how the protagonist, unintentionally left behind on Mars, kept striving by using all his wisdom to survive. And I like Matt Damon in the first place

The A-Team(TV Show)

I love how the A-Team, with their seemingly disparate personalities, use their individual abilities for a common goal: to take down the bad guys. The comedic elements and the resourceful weapon-making really appeal to my masculine side.
